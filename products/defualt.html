Products

KWIK-WALL’s customers are schools, church and religious institutions, convention centers, ballrooms, banks, condominiums, pool enclosures, lofts, and many other commercial and residential applications. KWIK-WALL’s history, going back to 1929, shows a dedication to quality, safety, and our strong emphasis on LEED projects. Our dedication to these efforts lead many architects, specification writers, contractors, and end users to request or specify one or many of our operable partitions products.

Operable Walls

Operable Partitions or movable walls are the standard product to use when dividing classrooms, training rooms, ball rooms, and gymnasiums. KWIK-WALL offers several models including steel framed panel, steel reinforced frames, hinged pair panels, individual panels, curve & diverter and electric wall type systems. [Product Specifications]

Movable Glass Walls

KWIK-WALL’s Movable Glass Walls are constructed with an industry first. We use a Mechanically Fastened System to attach the glass to the glass hardware. The glass hardware allows for stacking the glass walls in many configurations. [Product Specifications]
