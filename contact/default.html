Quality Service

KWIK-WALL's exclusive network of factory-trained field service technicians can repair ANY operable partition or movable wall system, regardless of the manufacturer. In addition to repairing and recovering wall partitions, we also provide maintenance and service contracts to fit your needs.

KWIK-WALL recommends that you perform regularly scheduled maintenance on your operable partition systems to keep them in good working order and to repair possible problems you may be unaware of. Proper maintenance ensures your operable partition system will provide excellent service year after year.

With service centers located throughout the United States, KWIK-WALL is able to respond to your operable partition emergency immediately.

To speak with a field service representative, please call 1-800-280-5945 or Email us at service@kwik-wall.com.
